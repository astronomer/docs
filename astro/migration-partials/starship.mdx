The [Astronomer Starship](https://pypi.org/project/astronomer-starship/) migration utility connects your source Airflow environment to your
 Astro Deployment and migrates your Airflow connections, Airflow variables, Environmental variables, and DAGs.

The Starship migration utility works as a [plugin](https://github.com/astronomer/starship/tree/master/astronomer-starship) with a user interface, or as an Airflow operator if you are migrating from a more restricted Airflow environment.
